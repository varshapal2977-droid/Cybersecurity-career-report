# Cybersecurity-career-report
•	Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, data, and digital infrastructure from cyberattacks. As technology continues to grow, cyber threats have also increased—ranging from malware and phishing to ransomware, identity theft, and data breaches.
•	In today’s digital world, almost every industry relies on secure systems, including banking, healthcare, education, government, and e-commerce. This has made cybersecurity one of the most important and fastest-growing fields in computing.
